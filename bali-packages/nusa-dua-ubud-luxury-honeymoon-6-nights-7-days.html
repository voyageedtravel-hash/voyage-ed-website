Frequently Asked

Questions & Answers

Do Indians need a visa for Bali?+
Indians get Visa-on-Arrival valid for 30 days. Cost is approximately ₹3,000 (USD 35) paid in cash at the airport.
What is the best time to visit Bali?+
April to October is ideal — dry, sunny weather perfect for beaches and outdoor activities. Avoid Jan-Feb (heavy rain) and Jul-Aug (peak crowds).
Is Bali safe for honeymoon couples?+
Absolutely. Bali is one of the most popular and safe honeymoon destinations for Indians. Most resorts have honeymoon-special inclusions.
Can the itinerary be customised?+
Yes! All our Bali packages are 100% customisable. Add days, change hotels, include flights, swap activities — just tell us your preferences.
