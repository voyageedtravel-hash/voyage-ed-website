Ideal 6-Day Georgia Itinerary from India

Day 1 — Arrival in Tbilisi

Fly from Delhi/Mumbai to Tbilisi (via Istanbul or Sharjah). Check in, explore Rustaveli Avenue. Tbilisi Old Town walk in the evening.

Day 2 — Tbilisi Full Day

Narikala Fortress, Sulfur Baths, Mtatsminda Park, Dry Bridge Market. Georgian dinner with live music.

Day 3 — Kazbegi (Stepantsminda)

Scenic drive to Kazbegi (2.5 hrs). Gergeti Trinity Church hike. Mount Kazbek views. Night in mountain guesthouse.

Day 4 — Mtskheta + Return

UNESCO World Heritage Jvari Monastery, Svetitskhoveli Cathedral. Return to Tbilisi.

Day 5 — Kakheti Wine Region

Day trip to Signagi (city of love), winery visits, wine tasting. Traditional Georgian feast.

Day 6 — Departure

Shopping at Tbilisi markets. Departure flight home.
