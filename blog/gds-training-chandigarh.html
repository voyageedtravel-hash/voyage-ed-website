What is GDS and Why Does It Matter for Your Career?

GDS (Global Distribution System) is the software backbone of the travel industry. Airlines, hotels, car rentals — all inventory is distributed through GDS platforms. Amadeus is the world's largest GDS, used by 90%+ of professional travel agencies.

💼 Who needs GDS skills? Travel consultants, airline staff, OTA agents, corporate travel managers, TMC executives, hotel reservation staff.

Without GDS training, you're limited to consumer-facing platforms like MakeMyTrip. With GDS certification, you access professional inventory, earn higher salaries, and qualify for jobs at top companies.
