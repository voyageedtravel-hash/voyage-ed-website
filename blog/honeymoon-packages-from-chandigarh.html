When to Book Your Honeymoon

Book your honeymoon 2–3 months before the wedding. This window gets you the best flight fares and hotel availability. Peak Indian wedding seasons (Nov–Feb, Apr–May) see Bali and Dubai prices rise 20–30% — early booking locks today's rates.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best honeymoon destination from Chandigarh under ₹1 lakh?

Bali (₹85,000/couple), Georgia (₹85,000/couple), Dubai (₹75,000/couple), and Thailand (₹75,000/couple) are the best honeymoon options under ₹1 lakh from Chandigarh, including flights, hotels, and romantic add-ons.

Which honeymoon destination is visa-free for Indians?

Georgia and Thailand are visa-free for Indian passport holders in 2026. Bali offers visa on arrival. These three are the easiest honeymoon destinations paperwork-wise.

Is Maldives worth it for honeymoon from India?

Yes — Maldives is the ultimate honeymoon destination with overwater villas and total privacy. From Chandigarh, packages start at ₹1,40,000/couple for 5 nights including seaplane transfers.

How far in advance should I book my honeymoon?

Book 2-3 months before your wedding date for the best fares. For peak season honeymoons (December, May), book 4 months ahead.
