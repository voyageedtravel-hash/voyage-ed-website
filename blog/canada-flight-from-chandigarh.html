❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cheapest flight from Chandigarh to Canada?

The cheapest Chandigarh to Canada fares route via Dubai or Istanbul, starting around ₹55,000 return in off-peak months (February-March, October-November). Direct routes via Delhi cost ₹58,000-95,000.

Is there a direct flight from Chandigarh to Canada?

No direct flights operate from Chandigarh (IXC). The nearest direct option is Amritsar (ATQ) to Toronto, seasonal on Air India. Most travellers connect via Delhi.

How much baggage do students get on Canada flights?

Student fares typically include 2-3 checked bags of 23kg each — one more than standard economy. Voyage-Ed arranges student fares with maximum baggage on Air India, Air Canada and British Airways.

When should I book my Canada flight from Chandigarh?

Book 2-3 months in advance for regular travel, and 4-5 months ahead for the September student intake when fares peak. February and October offer the lowest fares.
