Schengen Visa From India 2026 — Complete Step-by-Step Guide

✍ Vishal Sharma, Voyage-Ed Travels📅 2026-06-05⏱ 7 min read

Getting a Schengen visa is the gateway to 27 European countries — France, Germany, Italy, Spain, Switzerland, and more. At Voyage-Ed, we've helped hundreds of travelers from Chandigarh and Punjab get their Schengen visas approved. This is our complete, honest guide for 2026.

Schengen Visa Quick Facts 2026

  • Processing time: 15–25 working days (apply 6 weeks ahead)
  • Validity: Usually 30–90 days, single or multiple entry
  • Fee: €90 (₹8,000–₹8,500) + VFS service charges
  • Approval rate for Indians: 85–92% with proper documents
  • Best countries to apply through: France, Germany, Netherlands

Step-by-Step Schengen Visa Process

Step 1 — Choose the Right Consulate

Apply through the consulate of the country where you spend the most nights. If you're visiting multiple countries for equal nights, apply through the first entry country. For North India applicants, VFS Global centers in Chandigarh, Delhi, and Amritsar handle Schengen applications for most countries.

Step 2 — Book Appointment

Book your VFS appointment online — slots fill fast, especially in peak season (May–August). Book 8–10 weeks before travel. VFS Global website for your chosen country handles appointments.

Bank Balance — The Most Common Rejection Reason

Insufficient or unstable bank balance causes 60% of Schengen rejections from India. Key rules:

  • Show €50–100 per day of stay (₹4,500–₹9,000/day)
  • Funds must have been in account 3+ months — no sudden large deposits
  • Regular salary credits are the strongest signal
  • Fixed deposits and investments also help as supporting documents
  • Joint account statements acceptable if you're a signatory

FAQs — Schengen Visa India

How long does Schengen visa processing take from India?
15–25 working days for standard processing. Apply at least 6 weeks before travel. Express/fast-track processing (5–7 days) available at some consulates for additional fees. Peak season (May–August) takes longer — apply 8–10 weeks ahead.
