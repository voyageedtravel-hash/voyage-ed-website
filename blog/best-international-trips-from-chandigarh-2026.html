Which Airport Should Chandigarh Travelers Use?

This is the first question we get from Chandigarh travelers. The short answer: Amritsar (ATQ) for value, Delhi (DEL) for more options.

Chandigarh's own airport (IXC) has very limited international connectivity. Amritsar Airport is just 90 minutes by road and offers direct international flights to Dubai, Bangkok, Toronto, London, Kuala Lumpur, and several Southeast Asian cities. For destinations not served from Amritsar, driving to Delhi IGI (4–4.5 hours) unlocks the full spectrum of global routes.

For most destinations in this list, we'll tell you exactly which airport makes more sense — and how much the difference is.

Tips for Booking International Travel from Chandigarh

1. Book 6–10 Weeks Ahead for Best Prices

Flight prices from Amritsar and Delhi follow a consistent pattern — they're cheapest 6–10 weeks before departure, and prices spike in the last 2–3 weeks. For international flights, booking 8 weeks out typically saves ₹5,000–₹15,000 per person compared to last-minute fares.

2. Use Amritsar Airport More Than You Think

Most Chandigarh travelers default to Delhi for international flights, but Amritsar (just 90 minutes away) now has surprisingly good connectivity — direct flights to Dubai, Bangkok, Toronto, London, Singapore, Kuala Lumpur, and Birmingham. For these destinations, Amritsar saves you 4+ hours of travel and often comparable fares.

3. Visa-Free Countries First for First-Timers

If you're going international for the first time, choose a visa-free destination — Thailand, Georgia, Maldives, Nepal, or Bhutan. Skip the visa stress and paperwork. Once you've got a stamp in your passport and experience under your belt, Schengen and other complex visas become far less daunting.

4. Group Travel from Chandigarh = Massive Savings

We see this constantly — groups of 6–10 from Chandigarh who travel together save 20–35% on hotels and local transport compared to individual bookings. Voyage-Ed specializes in group packages and can negotiate significantly better rates for parties of 6+. WhatsApp us your group size and destination for a custom quote.

5. Travel Insurance Is Non-Negotiable

We've seen Chandigarh travelers skip travel insurance to save ₹800–₹2,000 and then face ₹2–5 lakh in medical expenses abroad. Buy travel insurance — it costs less than a restaurant meal in Europe and can save your entire trip.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which is the cheapest international trip from Chandigarh?
Thailand is consistently the cheapest — packages from ₹16,999 per person for 4N5D including flights, hotel, and transfers. Sri Lanka and Nepal are also under ₹20,000 per person for a full holiday package.
Which airport is best for international travel from Chandigarh?
Amritsar (ATQ) for direct flights to Dubai, Bangkok, Toronto, and London. Delhi IGI (DEL) for wider global connectivity. Chandigarh Airport (IXC) has very limited international routes and is not recommended for international travel.
Is Georgia visa-free for Indians in 2026?
Yes — Georgia has offered visa-free entry to Indian passport holders since 2023. You can stay up to 365 days without a visa. Just a valid passport and sufficient funds required.
What is the best time to travel internationally from Chandigarh?
October–March is the golden window. Chandigarh winters are cold, and international escapes to Thailand, Dubai, Bali, and Sri Lanka are at their best. April–June are also popular for Europe and Canada. July–September suits Georgia, Kazakhstan, and European destinations.
