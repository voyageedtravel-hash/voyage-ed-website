Ideal 5-Day Almaty Itinerary

Day 1 — Arrival in Almaty

Fly from Delhi to Almaty. Check in, evening stroll through Panfilov Park and the colourful Zenkov Cathedral.

Day 2 — Mountains Day

Shymbulak cable car, Medeu skating rink, and Big Almaty Lake viewpoint. Unforgettable Himalayan-style views.

Day 3 — City & Culture

Kok-Tobe hill, Green Bazaar, Almaty Tower, and local Kazakh cuisine tasting.

Day 4 — Charyn Canyon Day Trip

Full-day excursion to the "Grand Canyon of Kazakhstan" — dramatic red-rock formations.

Day 5 — Departure

Last-minute shopping for souvenirs and chocolates, then airport transfer.
