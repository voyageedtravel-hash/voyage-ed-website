Thailand is the most popular international destination for Indian travelers — and for good reason. It's visa-free, affordable, and endlessly exciting. But "how much does Thailand actually cost?" is the question we get asked the most at Voyage-Ed. This is our honest, detailed, up-to-date answer for 2026.

Quick Summary — Thailand Trip Cost From India 2026

  • Budget package (4N5D): ₹16,999–₹22,000/person (flights+hotel+transfers)
  • Mid-range (5N6D): ₹25,000–₹40,000/person

3. Daily Food & Transport Budget

Thailand is incredibly affordable once you're there. Here's a realistic daily budget in Thai Baht converted to INR (1 THB ≈ ₹2.4 in 2026):

  • Street food meal: ₹80–₹200 (Pad Thai, mango rice, som tam)
  • Restaurant meal (mid-range): ₹300–₹700 per person
  • Fine dining: ₹1,200–₹3,000 per person
  • Local transport (tuk-tuk, metro, taxi): ₹150–₹500/day
  • Bangkok–Pattaya bus: ₹200–₹400 one way
  • Bangkok–Phuket flight: ₹1,500–₹4,000 one way

Realistic daily budget: Budget traveler ₹1,500–₹2,500/day · Mid-range ₹3,000–₹5,000/day · Luxury ₹7,000–₹15,000/day

5. Visa — Free for Indians!

Thailand announced visa-free entry for Indian passport holders in November 2023, and it remains in effect through 2026. Key details:

  • Stay up to 30 days without any visa
  • No fee, no advance application required
  • Required: valid passport (6+ months validity), return ticket, hotel booking
  • For longer stays: Thailand Tourist Visa available (60 days, extendable)

6. Best Time to Visit Thailand from India

  • November–February (Peak): Best weather, 25–32°C, crowded, highest prices
  • March–May (Hot): Very hot (35–40°C), Songkran water festival in April, moderate prices
  • June–October (Monsoon): Rainy season, cheapest packages, 30–40% discount, some areas flooded
  • Best value with good weather: October and November
