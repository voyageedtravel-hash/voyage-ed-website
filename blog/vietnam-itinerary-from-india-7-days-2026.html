Perfect 7-Day Vietnam Itinerary

Day 1 — Arrive in Hanoi

Fly Delhi → Hanoi. Check in, explore the Old Quarter, try egg coffee, watch the night market come alive.

Day 2 — Hanoi City Tour

Hoan Kiem Lake, Temple of Literature, Ho Chi Minh Mausoleum, Train Street, and a water puppet show.

Day 3 — Ha Long Bay Cruise

Drive to Ha Long, board an overnight cruise. Kayaking, cave visits, sunset on deck. Unforgettable.

Day 4 — Ha Long → Da Nang

Morning cruise activities, return to Hanoi, fly to Da Nang. Evening at My Khe Beach.

Day 5 — Hoi An

Explore the lantern-lit ancient town, Japanese Covered Bridge, tailor shops, riverside lantern boat ride.

Day 6 — Ba Na Hills + Golden Bridge

Cable car to Ba Na Hills, the famous Golden Bridge held by giant stone hands, French Village.

Day 7 — Departure

Souvenir shopping in Da Nang, airport transfer home.
