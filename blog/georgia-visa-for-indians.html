Georgia Visa for Indians 2026 — Entry Rules Explained Simply

Georgia is one of the easiest countries for Indians to visit in 2026 — many Indian travellers qualify for visa-free entry, and the rest get a simple e-visa approved in days. Here's exactly how it works, without the confusion.

Who Gets Visa-Free Entry to Georgia

✅ Visa-FREE for Indians who hold: a valid visa or residence permit of USA, UK, EU/Schengen, Canada, Australia, Japan, South Korea, or GCC countries. Entry stamp on arrival, stay up to 90 days.

If you have a valid US B1/B2 visa, UK visa, Schengen visa, or UAE residence — you can fly to Georgia with NO additional visa. This covers a huge share of Indian travellers.

E-Visa for Everyone Else

No qualifying visa? Georgia's e-visa is among the world's simplest:

  1. Apply at evisa.gov.ge
  2. Fee: USD 25 (~₹2,100)
  3. Processing: 5 working days
  4. Validity: 120 days, 30-day stay
📋 Documents: passport scan, photo, hotel booking, return ticket, bank statement. Voyage-Ed files the complete application for every Georgia package client.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is Georgia visa-free for Indian citizens?

Indians holding a valid visa or residence permit of the USA, UK, EU, Canada, Australia, Japan, South Korea or GCC states enter Georgia visa-free for up to 90 days. Others need a simple e-visa (USD 25, 5 days processing).

How do I apply for Georgia e-visa from India?

Apply online at evisa.gov.ge with passport scan, photo, hotel booking and return tickets. Fee is USD 25, approval takes around 5 working days.

Can I enter Georgia with a UAE residence visa?

Yes — Indians with valid UAE (or any GCC) residence permits enter Georgia visa-free for up to 90 days.

How long can Indians stay in Georgia?

Visa-free entrants can stay up to 90 days. E-visa holders get 30 days within a 120-day validity window.
