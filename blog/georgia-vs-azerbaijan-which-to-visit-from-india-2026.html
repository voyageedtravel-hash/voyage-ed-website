Georgia vs Azerbaijan 2026: Which Should Indians Visit First?

By Vishal Sharma, Co-Founder Voyage-Ed · 2026-06-09 · 8 min read
💬 Get Free Quote →

Georgia and Azerbaijan are the two rising stars of the Caucasus — both affordable, both stunning, and both far easier to reach from India than most travellers realise. But which should you visit first? Here is an honest, side-by-side comparison for Indian travellers in 2026.

Quick Verdict

Choose Georgia first if you want visa-free entry, mountains and wine. Choose Azerbaijan if you want modern city glamour and something different. Better still — combine both in one Caucasus tour.

Georgia: The Highlights

Georgia's appeal is its variety packed into a small country — the cobbled lanes of old Tbilisi, the jaw-dropping Gergeti Trinity Church at Kazbegi, and the seaside city of Batumi. It is visa-free, vegetarian-friendly, and famously hospitable. For most Indians, Georgia is the easiest and most rewarding first Caucasus trip.

Azerbaijan: The Highlights

Azerbaijan, and especially Baku, feels like a different world — gleaming Flame Towers, the UNESCO-listed Old City, and day trips to mud volcanoes and the green hills of Gabala. The ASAN e-visa is simple, and Baku is one of the most photogenic cities in the region.

Why Not Both? The Combined Caucasus Tour

Because they share a border, many Voyage-Ed clients combine Georgia and Azerbaijan into a single 8-10 day journey — flying into Tbilisi and out of Baku (or vice versa). It is the most cost-effective way to experience the whole Caucasus in one trip.
