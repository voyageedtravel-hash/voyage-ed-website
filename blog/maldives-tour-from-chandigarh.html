Water Villa vs Beach Villa — Which to Choose?

Water villas are the iconic Maldives experience — waking up over the lagoon, direct ocean access from your deck. They cost 40–60% more than beach villas.

Smart strategy: Split your stay — 2 nights beach villa + 3 nights water villa. You save money AND get both experiences. Most Voyage-Ed Maldives packages are structured exactly this way.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cost of Maldives trip from Chandigarh?

A Maldives package from Chandigarh costs ₹70,000–₹1,40,000 per person for 4-5 nights including Delhi flights, resort stay, and transfers. Luxury overwater villa packages start around ₹1,40,000 per person.

Do Indians need a visa for Maldives?

No pre-arranged visa needed. Indians get a free 30-day visa on arrival in Maldives with a valid passport and return ticket.

What is the best time to visit Maldives?

November to April is the dry season with sunny skies — ideal but pricier. May to October offers 30-40% lower prices with short tropical showers.

Is Maldives cheaper than Bali?

No, Maldives is more expensive than Bali. A comparable Bali trip costs about half. But Maldives offers a unique overwater villa experience that Bali cannot match.
