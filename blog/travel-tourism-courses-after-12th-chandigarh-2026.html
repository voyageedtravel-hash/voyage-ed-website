What is GDS and Why It Matters

A Global Distribution System (GDS) like Amadeus is the engine travel agencies and airlines use to book flights, hotels, and packages. Mastering it is the single most valuable skill for a travel career — recruiters specifically look for hands-on GDS experience, which is exactly what our students gain.
