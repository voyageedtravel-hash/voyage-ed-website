5-Day Baku Itinerary for Indians

Day 1 — Arrival in Baku

Fly from Delhi via Istanbul/Dubai. Check-in, Baku Boulevard evening walk, Flame Towers light show.

Day 2 — Baku Old City & Modern City

Icheri Sheher (Old City), Maiden Tower, Shirvanshahs Palace. Afternoon: Highland Park, Heydar Aliyev Centre.

Day 3 — Fire & Mud Volcanoes Day Tour

Yanar Dag (eternal fire), Gobustan Petroglyphs (UNESCO), Mud Volcanoes. Absheron Peninsula.

Day 4 — Quba & Mountains

Optional: Day trip to Quba (carpet village) or Lahij (copper crafts). Or relax in Baku.

Day 5 — Shopping & Departure

Taza Bazaar for souvenirs, carpets. Departure flight.
