Vietnam Visa for Indians — E-Visa Guide 2026

✅ Vietnam e-visa for Indians: Apply online at evisa.xuatnhapcanh.gov.vn
  • Fee: USD 25 (approx ₹2,100)
  • Processing: 3 working days
  • Validity: 90 days, single or multiple entry
  • No physical visit required

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Do Indians need a visa for Vietnam?

Yes, Indians need an e-visa for Vietnam. Apply online at the official Vietnam e-visa portal. Fee is USD 25, approved in 3 working days. Voyage-Ed handles the visa process as part of your package.

What is the cost of Vietnam trip from India?

A complete Vietnam tour package from India costs ₹42,000–₹75,000 per person including flights, hotel, visa, and guided tours for 6-10 days.

What is the best time to visit Vietnam from India?

November to April is the best time — dry season, pleasant weather. Avoid May–October (monsoon season, especially in north Vietnam).

Can I visit Vietnam and Thailand in one trip?

Yes. A popular combo is Vietnam (Hanoi + Ha Long) + Thailand (Bangkok + Phuket) over 12 days. Voyage-Ed offers combined packages from ₹75,000 per person.

How many days are enough for Vietnam?

7-8 days covers the highlights: Hanoi, Ha Long Bay, and Hoi An. 10-11 days is needed for a full north-to-south experience including Ho Chi Minh City.
