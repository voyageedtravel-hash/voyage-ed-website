Is Thailand visa-free for Indians in 2026?

Bangkok or Phuket — which is better for first-time visitors?

First-time visitors should ideally do both. Bangkok for culture, temples, and food; Phuket for beaches and islands. A 7-day trip can comfortably cover both cities.

What is the best time to visit Thailand from India?

November to March is the best time for Thailand — dry weather, blue skies, and perfect beach conditions. Avoid May–October (monsoon season, especially in Phuket).
