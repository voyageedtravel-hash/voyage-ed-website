❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Which is the best summer holiday destination from India?

Kashmir (₹8,499) and Ladakh (₹13,999) are the best domestic summer escapes with 15-25°C weather. Internationally, Georgia (₹45,000) offers cool alpine weather and visa-free entry.

Where should I NOT travel from India in May-June?

Avoid Dubai (45°C+), most of Thailand (monsoon onset), and Rajasthan (extreme heat) during May-June. Head to mountains or northern-hemisphere temperate destinations instead.

When should I book summer holiday packages?

Book by March for May-June travel. School holiday demand pushes fares up 25-40% by April, and Kashmir/Ladakh hotels sell out for June.

Is Bali good in summer from India?

Yes — May onwards Bali enters its dry season with sunny 26-30°C days. It's actually one of the best times to visit Bali, before the July-August peak crowds.
