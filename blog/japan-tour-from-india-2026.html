Indians need a visa for Japan. The process is straightforward through VFS:

When is cherry blossom season in Japan?

Cherry blossoms peak from late March to early April in Tokyo and Kyoto. This is Japan's most popular (and expensive) season — book 4-5 months in advance.

Japan is a premium destination, but smart planning helps: JR rail passes, business hotels, and convenience-store meals keep costs manageable. Budget ₹8,000-12,000/day on ground.
