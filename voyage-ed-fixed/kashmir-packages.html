FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions about Kashmir

Best time to visit Kashmir?+
Mar-Jun for spring/tulips, Jul-Sep for cool summer, Oct-Nov for autumn colours, Dec-Feb for snow. Each season has its charm.
Is Kashmir safe currently?+
Yes. Tourist areas (Srinagar, Gulmarg, Pahalgam, Sonmarg) are safe and well-monitored. We follow daily updates from authorities.
Can elderly people do this trip?+
Yes, all sightseeing is by car. Pony rides and Gondola are optional. Houseboat stay is on still water — very comfortable.
