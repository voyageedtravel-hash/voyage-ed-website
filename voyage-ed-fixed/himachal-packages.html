Frequently Asked Questions about Himachal

Will I see snow?+
Dec-Feb guarantees snow. Mar-Apr at higher passes. Solang has year-round artificial snow activities.
Is Rohtang Pass open?+
May-Oct typically. Closed in winter. Daily permits limited — book early.
