FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions about Victoria Falls

When is most water?+
Mar-May post-rains: max water (1 million cubic feet/min). Spray reaches 400m. Sept-Dec less water but better swimming.
Devil's Pool?+
Natural infinity pool at edge of falls. Swim Sept-Dec (low water). Most thrilling experience on earth.
Best side?+
Both! Zambia 70% (closer, wet). Zimbabwe 30% (panoramic, drier). Doing both = complete experience.
