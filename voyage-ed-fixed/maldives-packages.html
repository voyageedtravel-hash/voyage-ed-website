FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions about Maldives

How do I reach my Maldives resort?+
Resorts are on private islands. Transfer is by speedboat (15-90 mins) for nearby resorts, or seaplane/domestic flight for far ones. We arrange this.
Is Maldives only for honeymooners?+
While famous for honeymoons, Maldives also offers family-friendly resorts and budget-friendly local-island stays in Maafushi.
Best time to visit Maldives?+
November to April — dry season with sunny days, calm seas, perfect for water activities.
